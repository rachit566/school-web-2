Stafford public school is an English medium school for boys and girls. During the session 2005 the school will conduct classes from nursery to 12. Subsequent years shall be addition for further classes S.P.S is established as the center for learning where the importance of discipline, duty and fair play is emphasized .The mission of our school is to translate substances to our vision of transformation of the self and society through an integrated formation with divine touch .The aim to establish institution is to make students self reliant, independent confident to take the challenge of life. STAFFORD SCHOOL PITH. was established in 2005 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Urban area. It is located in BIN block of PITHORAGARH district of Uttarakhand.
